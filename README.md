## About Me

I'm an Information Technology student at the **Polytechnic University of the Philippines – Taguig City** with hands-on experience in full-stack development, data analytics, and cybersecurity fundamentals. I've served as **Technical Lead** on multiple production systems — from school health management to organizational voting platforms — and I thrive at the intersection of clean code, practical data insights, and secure system design.

> *I don't just write code — I build systems that solve real problems for real people.*

- 🔭 Currently building REST APIs with **Java Spring Boot** and exploring **data pipelines**
- 📊 Focused on expanding skills in **Data Analytics, BI tools, and cloud-based data services**

- 🤝 Open to: Junior Dev roles, Data Analyst roles, Cybersecurity internships, and freelance projects

## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 🏥 PDMHS Student Medical System
> **Role:** Technical Lead & Backend Developer | **Client:** President Diosdado Macapagal High School

A secure, production-grade medical management system handling student health records, clinic consultations, and administrative workflows for school health personnel.

**What I built:**
- Centralized health records (medical history, allergies, immunizations)
- Consultation logging with diagnoses, treatments, and prescriptions
- Secure file management for medical documents with role-based access
- **Redis caching & session management** for performance optimization
- Queue-based background processing for heavy workloads
- Containerized deployment with **Docker & Laravel Sail**

**Tech:** `Laravel` `PHP 8.2+` `MySQL` `Redis` `Node.js` `Docker` `PHPUnit`
